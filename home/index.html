CyberSecurity

If I look at what I've learned now, ICT is just a big puzzle, it's all connected. There wasn't really a reason why I switched to this new topic, I just found it interesting. I like tinkering with things and then finding new things out. It's also mostly in the news when there's a new data breach or when a company got infected with ransomware. It was a bit confusing as when you search up an unknown term, you get an explanation with more unknown terms, kind of like a rabbit hole. I really found it interesting and went deeper and deeper. until I found a topic of how hacking/exploiting works, that was for me the cherry on the top. I switched to hacking and wanted to access the "Dark Web". In order to understand how you need to hack things, you first need to understand how they work, and even further, understand the source code better than the makers. But that is not always needed, as I started as a script kiddie and used already publicly made exploits. I installed and tried multiple hacker machines and tested some exploits. I thought, wow, this is powerful. But remember, with great power comes great responsibility. I went from website to software penetration testing and followed some good online courses. You could even get paid to hack other people's and companies' devices for money, which is called, and ethical hacker or a blue hat hacker. It was really cool and even hacked my dad's computer. And then I realized that nothing is actually safe. I was more aware of my privacy, which is a perfect reason to find out what the dark web actually is. It was really weird seeing all these things publicly available. The most dark webs are on the deep website, which just means you can't actually visit them with your normal browser like Chrome/Edge/Opera/Firefox/etc but have to use another secure private browser like Tor. While searching for all these things, I also found some quality Open Source Intelligences such as finding a person by picture, finding publicly accessible cameras and other devices, finding the person with address from a phone number, and more. After that, I made multiple malware and exploits along using other tools and specializing further on advanced cybersecurity.
